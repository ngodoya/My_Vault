# Repaso Métodos Abiertos
Newton-Rhapson Modificado: Multiplicidad independiente por newton rapshon

$u(x) = \frac{f(x)}{f'(x)}$

then

$x_{i+1}=x_i-\frac{u(x_i)}{u'(x_i)}$

with

$u'(x)=\frac{f'(x)^2-f(x)f''(x)}{[f'(x)]^2}$

simplified iteration formula:

$x_{i+1}=x_i-\frac{f(x_i)f'(x_i)}{[f'(x)]^2-f(x_i)f''(x_i)}$
